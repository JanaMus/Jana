Hi there! I´m Jana Musilova, a young researcher driven by a passion for unraveling the complexities of the biological world. My research is centered around computational biology, especially bioinformatics and systems biology. Throughout my scientific journey, I have been so far involved in various areas, ranging from processing sequencing data and annotating complex genomes to describing functional aspects of complex systems and inferring biological networks.
